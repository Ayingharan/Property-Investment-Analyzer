Documentation

Learn how to use the Property Investment Analyzer and understand its features

Getting Started
  • Upload your property dataset in CSV or Excel format on the Analyze Data page.
  • Configure analysis options and start the comprehensive analysis.
  • View results and download detailed reports.
  • Use the Single Prediction page for instant tax fairness assessment of individual properties.
  • Check Models for AI model status and architecture details.
Features
  • Tax Fairness Analysis: Detects anomalies in property tax assessments using AI.
  • Zoning Classification: Predicts and verifies property zoning designations.
  • Investment Risk Assessment: Evaluates investment risks for properties.
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Generates downloadable reports for your analyses.
FAQ

Q: What file formats are supported?
A: CSV, XLSX, and XLS files up to 100MB.

Q: How do I interpret the tax fairness results?
A: The system compares your current tax to a predicted fair tax and provides recommendations.

Q: Can I use my own AI models?
A: Yes, you can integrate custom models by updating the codebase.
